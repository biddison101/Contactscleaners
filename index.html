How it works

Clean in three steps

Step 01

Scan everything

Reads every contact from iCloud, Google, and work accounts. Finds duplicates using five matching methods — not just exact names. Groups three, four, or five copies together.

Step 02

See the full picture

Your Contact Health Score tells you exactly how clean your address book is. Every duplicate group and every incomplete contact is visible — nothing hidden behind a paywall.

Fix it in one tap

Merge duplicates with a single tap. Every action is previewed first, backed up automatically, and can be undone. Your contacts are always safe.
